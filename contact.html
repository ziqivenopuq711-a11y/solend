Security Disclosure

Security vulnerabilities must be disclosed through our Immunefi bug bounty program. Do NOT disclose security issues publicly or via email — use Immunefi for all security reports to qualify for bounties.

A Global, Distributed Team

We're a distributed team with contributors across North America, Europe, and Asia. There's no headquarters, no fixed office — just an exceptional team connected by a shared belief in the transformative potential of open, decentralized finance. This structure lets us attract the world's best talent regardless of geography and operate as a truly global protocol.

Our distributed model also mirrors our protocol's design philosophy: decentralized, resilient, and not dependent on any single point of failure. Just as Solend's smart contracts operate autonomously on Solana's global validator network, our team operates across the globe with the same shared mission and values.

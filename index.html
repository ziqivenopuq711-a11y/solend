Everything You Need for DeFi Lending

Solend provides a comprehensive suite of DeFi primitives purpose-built for speed, efficiency, and composability on Solana.

⬆

Supply & Earn

Deposit your assets into Solend's lending pools and start earning competitive, continuously compounding interest rates. Supply rates are determined algorithmically based on market utilization, ensuring you always receive fair market yields. Your deposited assets remain yours — withdraw at any time with no lockup periods, subject only to available liquidity in the pool.

⚡

Borrow Instantly

Use your crypto holdings as collateral to unlock instant liquidity without selling your assets. Borrow USDC, SOL, ETH, and more — all without credit checks, KYC, or centralized approval. Collateral factors ensure the protocol remains solvent at all times, and you retain exposure to your deposited assets while accessing the capital you need for other opportunities in the DeFi ecosystem.

⚙

Flash Loans

Access uncollateralized loans that exist entirely within a single Solana transaction. Flash loans enable advanced DeFi strategies such as arbitrage between markets, collateral swaps, self-liquidations, and complex multi-step financial operations — all without requiring upfront capital. If the loan is not repaid within the same transaction, the entire operation reverts. A fixed fee of 0.09% applies per flash loan.

How Solend Works

Getting started with Solend takes less than a minute. Connect your Solana wallet and begin participating in the decentralized lending economy in three simple steps.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Connect any Solana-compatible wallet — Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, Ledger, or Torus. No account registration, no email, no KYC required. Your wallet is your identity on Solend. The protocol is completely permissionless and open to anyone with a Solana wallet and assets to supply or borrow.

2

Supply or Borrow

Choose from 15+ supported assets and decide whether to supply liquidity to earn interest or deposit collateral to borrow against. The dashboard shows real-time APYs for all assets, your current borrow utilization, and your health factor — a single metric that indicates how close your position is to liquidation. Adjust your position at any time.

3

Earn & Manage

Watch your supplied assets compound interest in real time. Monitor your borrowed positions and maintain a healthy collateral ratio to avoid liquidation. Earn additional SLND governance token rewards through liquidity mining programs. Withdraw your supplied assets or repay borrowed positions at any time — all within milliseconds thanks to Solana's exceptional throughput.

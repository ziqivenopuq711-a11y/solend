Ecosystem

Building the Solana DeFi Ecosystem

Solend is deeply integrated with the wider Solana ecosystem, partnering with leading protocols across DEX aggregation, liquid staking, automated market making, and oracle infrastructure. Together, we are building a composable, interconnected financial system that rivals — and surpasses — the capabilities of traditional finance at a fraction of the cost and with orders of magnitude more transparency.

Partner Protocols

Solend works closely with the most important infrastructure projects in the Solana ecosystem to deliver a seamless, composable experience for users and developers alike.

🔀

Jupiter

DEX Aggregator

Jupiter is Solana's leading DEX aggregator, routing trades across all liquidity sources for the best price. Solend integrates with Jupiter to enable one-click collateral swaps — users can swap their collateral type directly within the Solend interface, powered by Jupiter's aggregation to ensure optimal execution at minimal slippage for any asset pair.

🌊

Marinade Finance

Liquid Staking

Marinade Finance is the leading liquid staking protocol on Solana, issuing mSOL tokens that represent staked SOL earning validator rewards. mSOL is supported as collateral on Solend, allowing users to simultaneously earn Marinade staking yields and use their mSOL to borrow additional assets — stacking yield sources without sacrificing liquidity or exposure.

🐋

Orca

AMM & Liquidity

Orca is Solana's most user-friendly AMM, known for its concentrated liquidity Whirlpools feature. Solend references Orca's price feeds as a secondary oracle source for certain assets and collaborates on liquidity strategies that enable users to provide concentrated liquidity on Orca while borrowing against their position on Solend for enhanced capital efficiency.

⚡

Raydium

Concentrated Liquidity

Raydium is one of Solana's oldest and most liquid AMMs, offering concentrated liquidity pools and yield farms. Solend collaborates with Raydium on incentive programs that reward users who supply assets to Solend and then deploy their borrowed assets as liquidity in Raydium pools, creating a composable yield loop that benefits both protocols' ecosystems.

🔥

Jito

MEV-Optimized Staking

Jito is a liquid staking protocol focused on MEV-optimized validator rewards, issuing JitoSOL tokens. JitoSOL earns additional yield from MEV capture compared to standard liquid staking. Solend supports JitoSOL as collateral, enabling users to borrow while their staked SOL compounds both base staking rewards and MEV tips simultaneously.

🔮

Pyth Network

Price Oracles

Pyth Network is Solend's primary oracle provider, delivering real-time, high-confidence price feeds directly from institutional market participants including Virtu Financial, Jane Street, and Jump Trading. Pyth's on-chain price feeds update every 400ms, enabling Solend to accurately value collateral positions and trigger liquidations with minimal delay during volatile market conditions.

Protocol Integrations

External protocols use Solend as an underlying infrastructure layer to build more sophisticated financial products and automated strategies for their users.

Yield Strategy Vaults

Yield aggregators and vault protocols integrate Solend to implement automated lending strategies. For example, a vault might supply user deposits to Solend to earn base interest, then use the cTokens as collateral to borrow more of the same asset and re-supply it — creating a leveraged yield loop that maximizes APY for depositors without requiring manual position management.

Collateral Leverage Loops

DeFi automation protocols build on Solend's primitives to offer one-click leveraged positions. A user wanting 3x long SOL exposure can supply SOL, borrow USDC, buy more SOL, and repeat — all in a single transaction using Solend's flash loans and Jupiter's swap aggregation. These composable leverage products make sophisticated strategies accessible to non-technical users.

Cross-Protocol Composability

Solend's open architecture means any Solana program can invoke its lending functions via Cross-Program Invocation (CPI). This enables seamless integration at the smart contract level — a protocol can atomically borrow from Solend, execute a trade on Orca, and repay the loan all in the same transaction, enabling financial products with a level of composability not possible in traditional finance.

DeFi Composability — Money Legos on Solana

The concept of "money legos" describes how DeFi protocols can be composed together like building blocks to create new financial products that would be impossible in isolation. Solend is a foundational money lego in the Solana DeFi stack — a lending primitive that other builders can use as a component in their own products.

Solend's open-source smart contracts are permissionlessly accessible by any program on Solana. The SDK and comprehensive developer documentation make it easy for teams to integrate Solend's lending capabilities in days rather than months. There are no gated API keys, whitelists, or integration fees for on-chain integrations.

This open architecture has already spawned an ecosystem of protocols building on top of Solend — from simple yield optimizers to complex structured products. Every new integration deepens liquidity and strengthens the Solana DeFi ecosystem as a whole.

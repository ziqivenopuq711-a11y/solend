Roadmap

Building the Future of DeFi on Solana

From a small team with a vision to one of Solana's most critical infrastructure protocols, Solend's journey has been defined by continuous innovation, community-led development, and an unwavering focus on security. This roadmap reflects our past achievements and our ambitious plans for what comes next — a more connected, efficient, and accessible global lending market.

Past Milestones

From our initial mainnet launch to crossing $1B in TVL, every milestone reflects the trust our community has placed in Solend and the work of our contributors worldwide.

Q3 2021

Mainnet Launch

Solend v1 launches on Solana mainnet with support for 5 initial assets. The protocol processes its first $10M in deposits within 48 hours, validating demand for lending on Solana.

Q4 2021

SLND Token Launch

The SLND governance token launches with the first liquidity mining program. Over 15,000 wallets participate in the initial distribution event. On-chain governance goes live with the first community proposals.

Q1 2022

Isolated Pools

Isolated lending pools launch, allowing long-tail assets to be listed safely. The first isolated pools support ORCA and MNGO tokens. Solend TVL crosses $500M as DeFi summer arrives on Solana.

Q2 2022

Flash Loans

Uncollateralized flash loans go live, enabling arbitrage bots and liquidation strategies on Solend. Usage grows rapidly, with flash loan volume reaching $50M+ within the first month of availability.

Q3 2022

Mobile App v1

Solend launches a native mobile application for iOS and Android, making DeFi lending accessible on-the-go. The app supports all core protocol features and integrates with Phantom and Solflare mobile wallets.

Q4 2022

$1B TVL Milestone

Solend crosses $1 billion in total value locked, cementing its position as the leading lending protocol on Solana. The milestone is celebrated with a community airdrop to active users who contributed to early protocol growth.

Q1 2023

Cross-Chain Bridges

Integration with Wormhole and Portal Bridge enables users to supply assets bridged from Ethereum, BNB Chain, and other networks. ETH and WBTC lending pools see significant inflows from cross-chain capital.

Q3 2023

Trail of Bits Audit

Solend completes its fourth comprehensive security audit with Trail of Bits, covering the v2 architecture. Zero critical or high findings — the cleanest audit in the protocol's history and a testament to the engineering team's security culture.

In Progress

Current Development (2024)

The team is actively shipping multiple major improvements in 2024, focused on protocol security, user experience, and institutional readiness.

In Development

Solend v2 Architecture

A major redesign of the core lending program with improved account layout, more efficient compute unit usage, and enhanced composability for CPI integrations. v2 reduces transaction costs by up to 40% for standard operations.

In Development

Improved Liquidation Engine

A new liquidation engine with support for partial liquidations, dynamic liquidation bonuses, and MEV-resistant liquidation ordering. The new engine processes liquidations more efficiently and distributes a portion of liquidation profits back to affected borrowers.

In Development

Enhanced Oracle System

A more robust oracle aggregation layer that combines Pyth Network, Switchboard, and Chainlink feeds with configurable staleness and confidence thresholds per asset. Reduces single-oracle dependency risk significantly.

In Development

Mobile App v2

A complete redesign of the Solend mobile app with real-time push notifications for health factor warnings, a transaction history dashboard, DeFi strategy wizards, and support for advanced features like flash loans and collateral swaps.

In Development

New Asset Listings

Governance proposals to add JLP (Jupiter LP token), PYUSD, and several other high-demand assets to both main and isolated pools. New asset listings are evaluated against Solend's risk framework for oracle quality, liquidity depth, and volatility profile before listing.

In Development

Analytics Dashboard v2

A rebuilt analytics dashboard showing real-time TVL, utilization rates, APY history, liquidation events, and user position health distributions. The new dashboard will be open-source and embeddable by third-party platforms.

Future Plans (2025 and Beyond)

Looking further ahead, Solend is exploring transformational new capabilities that will expand the protocol's reach and redefine what on-chain lending can achieve.

Cross-Chain Lending

Enable users to supply assets on Ethereum and borrow on Solana, or vice versa, through a unified cross-chain lending interface built on Wormhole messaging. Cross-chain lending expands Solend's addressable market by an order of magnitude, tapping into the vast liquidity pools of Ethereum DeFi users who want access to Solana's speed and cost advantages.

Institutional Lending Desk

A dedicated institutional interface with higher credit limits, OTC borrow arrangements, custom risk parameters, and compliance-ready reporting tools. The institutional desk will serve crypto funds, market makers, and treasury management operations seeking the efficiency of on-chain lending without the constraints of retail-focused interfaces.

Undercollateralized Loans

Research into on-chain credit scoring, reputation systems, and DeFi identity to enable undercollateralized lending for verified users and protocols. This is a major frontier for DeFi — reducing the capital inefficiency of overcollateralized loans while maintaining protocol safety through novel risk assessment mechanisms.

Real-World Asset Integration

Partnerships with RWA tokenization platforms to enable US Treasuries, tokenized real estate, and other off-chain assets to be used as collateral on Solend. RWA collateral unlocks an enormous new capital base for DeFi borrowers who hold traditional financial assets and want to access on-chain liquidity without selling their holdings.

Our Vision

Solend's long-term vision is to become the foundational lending primitive for all of DeFi — not just on Solana, but across every major blockchain ecosystem. We believe that open, permissionless credit markets are essential infrastructure for the global financial system of the future, just as roads and ports are essential infrastructure for physical trade.

By building on the most performant blockchain infrastructure available, partnering with every major DeFi ecosystem, and continuously improving through community governance, Solend will evolve from a Solana lending protocol into a universal financial primitive — the lending layer of the decentralized internet.

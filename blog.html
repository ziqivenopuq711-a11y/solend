Protocol Update November 14, 2024

Solend V2: A New Era for DeFi Lending

After 18 months of development, extensive internal testing, and a comprehensive audit by Trail of Bits, we are thrilled to announce the upcoming launch of Solend V2 — a complete architectural overhaul of the lending protocol that sets the foundation for the next decade of decentralized lending on Solana.

V2 introduces a new account model that reduces per-transaction compute unit consumption by up to 40%, an upgraded liquidation engine that is more efficient and fairer to borrowers, and a revamped oracle aggregation system with configurable staleness and confidence thresholds per asset. Together, these improvements dramatically enhance both the user experience and the protocol's resilience under adverse market conditions.

The V2 smart contracts will be deployed via a governance proposal that allows existing users to migrate their positions through an assisted migration flow. No user action is required for the migration — the protocol will handle position migration automatically for all active obligations. Liquidity will be seamlessly transferred from v1 to v2 pools with zero downtime.
